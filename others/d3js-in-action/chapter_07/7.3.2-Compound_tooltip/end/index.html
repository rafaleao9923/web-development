Visualizing 40 Years of Music Industry Sales

This project visualizes the music sales in the United States by format between 1973 and 2019. These formats range from physical supports like vinyl, 8-track, cassette, and cd, to music consumed digitally, with downloads and streaming services. The category other includes less prominent sources of revenue like music videos (physical), synchronization, and royalties.
Move your mouse over the graph to see the breakdown of sales for each year.

Data Source: MakeoverMonday 2020/W21
