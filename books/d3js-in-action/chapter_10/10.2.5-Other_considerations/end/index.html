Cetacean species

This visualization shows a collection of cetacean species. Each circle represents a cetacean. The circles' position on the scatterplot reveals their size and estimated population. The area of the circles is proportional to the weight of each cetacean. Their color represents the conservation status of each species.

How to read this visualization

Color: Conservation statuses

Area of the circle: Weight of the cetaceans

Select a group of cetaceans

Pass your mouse over the circles or access them with the tab key to discover the cetacean they represent.

Data sources

  • CWAzores
  • Wikipedia

Method

The population estimate of some cetaceans was provided as a range. In these cases, the average value is shown on the visualization.

The conservation statuses are defined by IUCN (International Union for Conservation of Nature).
